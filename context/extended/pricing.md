## Pricing Model

(Describe your pricing structure. Example:)
(- Freemium with paid tiers)
(- Free tier: 3 projects, 5 team members, basic features)
(- Pro: $29/mo per workspace — unlimited projects, client portal, integrations)
(- Business: $79/mo per workspace — advanced reporting, SSO, priority support)
(- Enterprise: custom pricing)

## Pricing Philosophy

(What principles guide your pricing decisions? Example:)
(- Price on value delivered, not features counted)
(- Keep the free tier genuinely useful — no crippled experience)
(- Per-workspace, not per-seat — don't penalize growing teams)
(- Annual discount: 20% — reward commitment without heavy pressure)

## How to Present Price

(How should pricing be framed in sales and marketing? Example:)
(- Lead with the free tier — remove risk)
(- Compare to cost of alternatives (hiring, enterprise tools))
(- Frame as per-project cost, not monthly expense)
(- Show ROI: "pays for itself after one project")
